# She Bakes Sourdough

- **Handle:** @shebakesourdough
- **Creator:** Jade Lim
- **Primary platform:** Instagram
- **Cross-platform:** TikTok presence, website
- **Niche / sub-niche:** Baking / sourdough

## Scale and trajectory

- Instagram: ~2M followers, 1,080+ posts.
- The largest sourdough-specific Instagram account currently identifiable. Borderline classification: the author exists and is named, but the format is overwhelmingly hands-on-dough rather than face-on-camera, so the format is replicable by a brand channel.

## Format

Instagram Reels of dough being kneaded, shaped, scored, baked, sliced. The camera is on the dough almost exclusively. Hands enter frame; face appears occasionally, never as the focal point. Carousel posts walk through a recipe in 5–10 frames. Static finished-loaf photographs anchor the feed grid.

Caption style is informational rather than confessional — recipe ratios, technique notes, troubleshooting. The brand voice is friendly but not performative.

## Engagement signals

Sourdough Instagram is a self-contained community with high engagement rates relative to broader food Instagram. Saves run high because each post is a reference for a future bake.

## Standout post patterns

- The "before/after" Reel — unscored shaped loaf, score, oven spring.
- The "cross-section" carousel — finished loaf, then slice cuts revealing the crumb structure.
- The "hydration test" — same flour at different hydrations, side-by-side comparison.

## What Homemade can take

- **Bread Instagram works without a face.** The format is the proof. Homemade can step into this sub-category with the brand-direction styling and compete on visual identity rather than face recognition.
- **High-saves-rate content is the goal.** Sourdough scoring posts, hydration comparisons, troubleshooting carousels — all of these earn saves and bookmarks because they're reference material. The Homemade bookmark feature (already shipped in Phase 4) is the structural fit.
- **Carousel as a tutorial medium.** Instagram carousels are an underused format outside food Instagram. The Homemade illustration style suits the carousel format directly.

## What we'd do differently

- Brand-direction styling. She Bakes Sourdough is functional in its set-up — practical kitchen, simple worktop. Homemade should style with the linen / ceramic / sage palette consistently across every post.
- Caption length. Many bread Instagram captions run long because the recipe is in the caption. Homemade keeps captions short and links to the full tutorial — captions point to homemade.education, recipes live on the site.
