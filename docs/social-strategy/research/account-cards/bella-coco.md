# Bella Coco

- **Handle:** Bella Coco Crochet
- **Creator:** Sarah-Jayne Fragola (UK)
- **Primary platform:** YouTube
- **Cross-platform:** Instagram, blog, pattern shop
- **Niche / sub-niche:** Crochet / tutorials, basics, follow-alongs

## Scale and trajectory

- YouTube: ~1.9M subscribers.
- One of the largest English-language crochet YouTube channels. UK-based, which makes the voice a useful reference for Homemade's British audience.

## Format

Hands-and-yarn camera framing. Voice-over narration. Bright clean production. Tutorials are walked through step by step — slow and accessible, designed for absolute beginners as much as established crocheters.

Sarah-Jayne's face appears occasionally in introductions but the format is structurally hands-led. The audio narration is the dominant voice element, not the on-camera presence. Borderline classification — the format is replicable by a brand-channel narrator, even if a different voice carries it.

## Engagement signals

High retention on long tutorials. Strong saves on pattern videos because the audience returns to follow along while crocheting.

## Standout video patterns

- The "first project" walk-through — a complete beginner project in one video, no skips.
- The stitch dictionary — a single stitch demonstrated in detail with multiple-angle views.
- The pattern follow-along — a complete pattern realised step by step over 30–60 minutes, with chapter markers.

## What Homemade can take

- **The hands-and-yarn-with-voice-over format is the working crochet template.** Homemade can replicate this exactly: camera over the work, generic narration carrying the instruction, illustrated overlays for stitch diagrams.
- **British voice as positioning.** Most large crochet YouTube channels are US-based (The Crochet Crowd, Naztazia). A British-rooted brand channel has a clear positioning opportunity.
- **Long-form pattern follow-alongs as a content category.** A 45-minute video of a granny-square blanket being made in real time is the kind of high-retention content the YouTube algorithm rewards.
- **The beginner audience is the largest sub-audience.** Homemade's beginner mode is already shipped in Phase 4 — a structural fit for the crochet content strategy.

## What we'd do differently

- Brand-direction styling. Bella Coco's set-up is bright and functional; Homemade's would use the linen / sage / cream palette with the locked typography overlays.
- Generic narration rather than personal narration. The brand-channel voice for Homemade crochet would be editorial rather than personal — the format is replicable without depending on a specific narrator's recognisability.
