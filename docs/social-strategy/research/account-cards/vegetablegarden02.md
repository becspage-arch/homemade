# vegetablegarden02 (Horta)

- **Handle:** @vegetablegarden02
- **Primary platform:** TikTok
- **Cross-platform:** Limited public footprint beyond TikTok.
- **Niche / sub-niche:** Gardening / vegetable, faceless

## Scale and trajectory

- TikTok: ~1.1M followers.
- Bio listed as "Horta" (the Portuguese word for vegetable garden). The faceless format at scale — a million-plus TikTok followers without a presenter is the proof point for the niche.

## Format

Overhead and three-quarter angle shots of vegetable beds, time-lapses of growth, hands harvesting, close-ups of plants. No face, no voice-over. Music is light. On-screen captions are short and informational.

Content cycles with the growing season — sowing in spring, training and feeding in summer, harvest in autumn, soil and bed prep in winter.

## Engagement signals

Audience is genuinely gardening-curious rather than aesthetic-only. Comments are technique-led (variety questions, pest identification, climate context). Reposts and saves are high during sowing windows.

## Standout post patterns

- The time-lapse — seedling to mature plant in a 15-second clip.
- The "what to sow this week" overhead — a hand placing seeds into module trays with the variety name on screen.
- The harvest haul — a basket filling shot, captioned with weights.

## What Homemade can take

- **Faceless vegetable gardening can reach 1M+ on TikTok.** The proof is here. The format is the same as overhead cooking, applied to the garden surface.
- **The seasonal cadence is its own content calendar.** Sowing, growing, feeding, harvesting, preserving — the year writes the brief.
- **Time-lapse is the gardening equivalent of ASMR.** The visible change over weeks is what gardening TikTok rewards. Homemade can set up time-lapse capture once per crop and earn months of posts from each.

## What we'd do differently

- British seasonality and varieties. The audience for British gardening on TikTok is large and currently under-served by UK-rooted accounts. Sowing dates, recommended varieties, climate notes should be British by default.
- Brand-direction styling — terracotta pots, sage tones in the framing, illustration overlays for plant identification. Vegetablegarden02 is functional in its set-up; Homemade should be visibly Homemade.
- A linked tutorial. Each TikTok should drive to the deep tutorial on homemade.education ("Sowing tomatoes from seed: the full guide").
