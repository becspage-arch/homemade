# ikneadbread

- **Handle:** @ikneadbread
- **Primary platform:** TikTok
- **Cross-platform:** Limited — TikTok-led account.
- **Niche / sub-niche:** Baking / sourdough, decorative scoring

## Scale and trajectory

- TikTok: ~189K followers as of late 2025.
- Mid-scale faceless account — useful as a case study of the format working below the multi-million-follower tier.

## Format

Hands-only, overhead. Sourdough kneading, shaping, and decorative scoring. Each post focuses on a single loaf and a single scoring pattern. Audio is the dough sound itself — "the only sound comes from squishing the crunchy crust" was how one source described a viral post. Music is light or absent.

## Engagement signals

The sourdough community on TikTok is engaged and committed. Comments tend to be technique-led: hydration questions, flour types, oven setups. Saves and shares are high relative to follower count because each scoring pattern is a reference for future bakes.

## Standout video patterns

- The "score reveal" — overhead shot of an unscored shaped loaf, the lame enters frame, slow precise cuts, oven-spring close-up after baking.
- The "ear" close-up — extreme detail on the open crust ear after the score has bloomed.
- The "compare" — two loaves side by side with different scoring patterns, same dough.

## What Homemade can take

- **The format works at mid-scale.** A 189K-follower account is a viable business in TikTok's economics, and the production cost per video is genuinely small — one hour of baking produces multiple posts.
- **A single technique can sustain a content channel.** Sourdough scoring alone is enough subject matter to build to six figures of followers.
- **The audience is genuinely teach-able.** Sourdough TikTok rewards real precision, not theatrical shortcuts. This aligns with the Homemade depth promise.

## What we'd do differently

- Decorative scoring is one of many sourdough technique surfaces. Homemade would pair it with the long-form tutorial coverage (hydration, autolyse, bulk fermentation, shaping) and use the TikTok scoring posts as the discovery surface that funnels to the full tutorial.
- Brand-direction styling: linen napkin under the cutting board, sage and cream palette, ceramic proofing bowl. Ikneadbread's current styling is functional rather than branded.
