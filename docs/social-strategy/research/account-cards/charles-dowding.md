# Charles Dowding

- **Handle:** Charles Dowding 1nodig (YouTube)
- **Primary platform:** YouTube
- **Cross-platform:** Instagram (~half a million followers), own site charlesdowding.co.uk (books, courses, online learning), Substack-adjacent newsletter
- **Niche / sub-niche:** Gardening / no-dig vegetable growing

## Scale and trajectory

- YouTube: ~640–656K subscribers.
- Instagram: ~500K followers.
- Author of 14 books. Teaches at Homeacres market garden in Somerset and runs an online learning platform.

## Why this card exists

Charles Dowding is included as a borderline / format-reference card, not as a brand-channel replication target.

He is on camera — his face, voice, and 42 years of experience are the draw. By the strict filter of this research, he's out of scope.

The format he has built, however, is structurally replicable without him:

- The slow garden walk, narrated by an expert voice.
- Hands in the soil, demonstrating technique.
- Educational framing — technique-led, not entertainment-led.
- A consistent garden (Homeacres) as the visual anchor.
- Calm pacing, no over-editing, no music tricks.

A Homemade brand-channel narrator delivering the same kind of content (calm authority, hands-in-the-soil demonstrations, walks through a beautifully styled garden, or in Homemade's case an illustrated montage) could occupy structurally similar territory without depending on Charles's specific recognisability.

## What Homemade can take

- **The slow garden walk as a content format.** Homemade can build this with a combination of garden b-roll (filmed at allotments or borrowed gardens, with rights cleared), illustrations of the techniques, and generic narration.
- **The Somerset / English-countryside aesthetic.** Charles's garden is in Somerset. The aesthetic is the rural Englishness Homemade aspires to. Visual references for the illustration style can pull from this directly.
- **Long-form respect for the audience.** Charles's videos run 15–30 minutes and audiences watch. The bar for length is higher than mainstream YouTube assumes when the content is genuinely informative.
- **The no-dig position is a strong opinion.** Homemade's gardening section can have similarly strong opinions (companion planting, water management, seed-saving) that anchor the content rather than hedging.

## What we'd do differently

- Editorial-voice narration rather than Charles's personal narration. Homemade's gardening voice is editorial.
- Illustration overlays for technique diagrams. Charles uses minimal graphic overlay; Homemade's illustration-first identity layers in over the garden footage.
