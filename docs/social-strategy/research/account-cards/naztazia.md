# Naztazia

- **Handle:** Naztazia
- **Creator:** Donna Wolfe
- **Primary platform:** YouTube
- **Niche / sub-niche:** Multi-craft (crochet, knitting, gardening, sewing, beading)

## Scale and trajectory

- YouTube: ~2.3M subscribers.
- One of the largest multi-craft brand-channel-style accounts in the textile space. Active since the late 2000s; cumulative library is enormous.

## Format

Hands-and-craft camera framing with voice-over. Tutorial-led across multiple craft categories. The face appears occasionally but the format is structurally hands-led. Borderline classification: Donna has built personal recognition over more than a decade, but the format is replicable.

## Engagement signals

Multi-craft positioning means cross-category subscriber acquisition. A viewer who arrives for a crochet tutorial may return for a knitting one or a gardening one. This is structurally the Homemade play — one trusted publisher across all the homemaking categories.

## Standout video patterns

- The multi-craft library structure. Playlists organise the catalogue by craft and by skill level.
- Beginner-first content as a foundational layer. A new visitor can find a "first scarf" or "first granny square" tutorial easily.
- Pattern + product loop — many videos link to free patterns on the Naztazia website, which builds the email list and the SEO authority.

## What Homemade can take

- **Multi-craft brand-channel works at scale.** Naztazia is the proof that a single voice can credibly span crochet, knitting, gardening, sewing, and more without diluting trust. This is the single best validation of Homemade's "one trusted publisher across categories" thesis.
- **The library compounds.** Donna has been publishing for over a decade. The cumulative effect is what makes the channel an authority. Homemade's tutorial volume (1,500–3,000 target) is the digital equivalent.
- **The free-pattern hook drives email signups.** The tutorial-to-pattern-to-email funnel is well-tested. Homemade's bookmarks and projects in Phase 4 are the structural equivalent.

## What we'd do differently

- Brand-direction styling. Naztazia is functional in its visual register; Homemade should be visibly editorial.
- Editorial voice rather than personal voice. The Homemade brand channel can replicate the multi-craft span with an editorial register that doesn't depend on a single narrator's personality.
