# Cold Salad Explosion With Quesadillas

> source: master.docx · section: LUNCHES · Serves 4

## Ingredients

- Salad
- ½ onion
- 1 clove garlic
- ½ red pepper
- ½ green pepper
- ½ yellow pepper
- ½ cucumber
- 3 tomatoes

### French dressing

- Quesadillas
- Cheese or vegan cheese
- Flour tortilla

## Method

Salad

Dice peppers, cucumber and tomatoes

Mix in bowl

Sprinkle with french dressing and serve.

Quesadillas

Dice 3 chicken breasts

Sprinkle grated cheese over a flour tortilla

Cover with another tortilla.

Repeat to make a total of 2 tortilla sandwiches

Grill each one on both sides till the cheese is melted.

Cut each one into 6 slices and put in oven till ready to serve
