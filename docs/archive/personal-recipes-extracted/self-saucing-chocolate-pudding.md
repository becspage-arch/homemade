# Self Saucing Chocolate Pudding

> source: master.docx · section: DESSERTS

## Ingredients

- 100gm butter or vegan butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 2 tbsp cocoa powder
- 1 ⅓ cup flour
- 4 tsp baking powder
- ¼ tsp salt
- ⅔ cup milk or almond milk
- ½ tsp vanilla essence
- 1 cup brown sugar
- ½ cup white sugar
- 4 tbsp cocoa powder
- 2 cup boiling water

## Method

Turn the oven on to 180C bake.

Melt the butter and mix in the sugar and cocoa powder.

Add flour, baking powder and salt and mix until just combined.

Mix in milk and vanilla.

Put into a dish so it comes no more than 1/3 up the side (cos it boils, and will mess up your oven) 20x25cm is good.

Put brown and white sugar, and cocoa powder on top.

Just before it goes into the oven carefully pour the boiling water over a spoon onto the mixture.

Bake for 40 minutes at 180C.

Serve immediately with whipped cream.
