# Salmon Teriyaki

> source: master.docx · section: DINNERS · (serves 2)

## Ingredients

- 2x salmon filets (skin removed)
- Approx 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp maple syrup (or honey can substitute)
- 1 clove crushed or finely chopped garlic
- ¼ chopped onion
- Optional - Sesame seeds to serve

## Method

Mix soy sauce, maple syrup, garlic and onion together in a small oven dish.

Put the salmon in a shallow dish and spoon the marinade over it. The salmon should fill the dish and the marinade come about ½ way up the salmon so it doesn’t dry out during cooking. If it doesn’t, I’ll often just add a little extra soy sauce.

Leave for 10-30 minutes to marinate.

Bake at gas 7 for about ½ hour. It should be cooked after around 15mins but you’re looking for the top of the salmon to caramelise.
