# Limoncello Curd

> source: master.docx · section: BAKING + TREATS

## Ingredients

- 200 ml lemon juice
- 2-3 lemons, finely grated zest only
- 125 g salted butter, softened and diced
- 450 g granulated sugar
- 4 large free range eggs, beaten (approximately 200ml)
- 50 ml limoncello liqueur

## Method

1. Preheat the oven to 130C/110Cfan/gas1. Put 2-3 200g jam jars in the oven for 15 minutes to sterilise them. 2. Add the lemon juice, lemon zest, butter and sugar to a heatproof bowl placed over a pan of simmering water. 3. Once the butter has melted – the mixture will be thick and glossy –, add the beaten egg through a sieve and whisk with a balloon whisk or wooden spoon until the curd is thick and creamy, approx ten minutes. It should coat the back of a spoon. When you’re ready to pot add the limoncello to the curd and then straight into the jars.

Cooks Tips...

On no account must the fruit mixture boil or it may curdle. Store in a cool, dark place and use within 4 weeks.
