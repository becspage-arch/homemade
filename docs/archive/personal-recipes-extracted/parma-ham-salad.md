# Parma Ham Salad

> source: master.docx · section: LUNCHES · (serves 2)

## Ingredients

- Salad leaves
- Red onion
- Goats cheese
- 4-6x slices of Parma Ham
- 1x Red pepper
- French vinaigrette dressing or olive oil

## Method

Dice red pepper

Drizzle salad leaves with french dressing or olive oil

Combine and serve
