# Salmon Au Page

> source: master.docx · section: DINNERS

## Ingredients

- Salmon (or Cod)
- Seasoned flour for dusting
- Celery
- 1x pepper (any colour)
- 1x red onion
- Sliced mushrooms
- Crème fraiche, hollandaise or Oatly vegan cream fraiche
- Optional: Roasted vine tomatoes
- Optional: Pasta for more bulk

## Method

Fish in pan and fry until cooked

Dust fish with seasoned flour

Keep fish warm

Fry veggies until soft

Add crème fraiche or hollandaise to veggies until cooked

Put veggies to one side.  Put salmon back in the pan to reheat.
