# Slow Cooker Italian Chicken Pasta

> source: master.docx · section: DINNERS

## Ingredients

- 1 onion chopped
- 2 cloves garlic minced
- 1.25 pounds chicken tenders
- salt and pepper to taste
- 25 oz marinara sauce one jar
- 12 oz cream cheese
- 1 tsp Italian Seasoning (or make your own using basil, oregano and thyme)
- 2 bay leaves
- 1 lb pasta

## Method

Add diced onion, garlic, chicken breasts, cream cheese, marinara sauce, and some seasonings into a cold crockpot.

Cover and cook on high for 3-4 hours. If your crockpot runs hot, you might want to check the chicken for doneness at 2 1/2 hours. Also make sure to know how

Once the chicken is cooked, remove, shred and return back to the crockpot. At this point remove the bay leaves as well. Stir to melt cream cheese into sauce completely.

Cook pasta in a pot on the stove.

Toss with freshly cooked pasta and serve.

TIP - This chicken pasta recipe calls for cream cheese. If left too long in the crockpot at too high of a heat, it will curdle. The same goes for milk, cream, or greek yoghurt. If you’re worried about curdling because your crockpot runs hot, try cooking at the low setting for a longer period of time OR stir the dairy in at the end. For this recipe, once you shred the chicken and add it back into the marinara, stir in the cream cheese and melt it into the sauce. It should work just as well!
