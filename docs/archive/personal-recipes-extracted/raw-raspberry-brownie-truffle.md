# Raw Raspberry Brownie Truffle

> source: master.docx · section: BAKING + TREATS

## Ingredients

- ½ cup (85g) Walnuts
- ½ cup (73g) Almonds
- ½ cup (50g) Oats
- 4tbsp (40g) Cocoa powder
- A pinch of salt
- 3tbsp Agave syrup
- ½ cup (85g )Dates, soak in ho t water for 15 minutes
- ½ cup freeze dried raspberry

## Method

Place freeze dried raspberry into blender, blend into fine powder, set aside for later use

Place walnuts, almonds, oats, cocoa powder and salt into blender, pulse until coarsely chopped, place into a mixing bowl

Place soaked dates and agave syrup into blender, blend until smooth

Place chopped oat-nut mixture into blender with dates-agave paste, blend to form a dough (don’t worry if it’s a little wet or if it’s too dry feel free to add abit more agave)

Remove dough from blender, shape into balls, place into fridge for 1 hour to harden

After 1 hour remove balls from fridge then roll brownie balls on raspberry powder, until the ball is entirely covered with raspberry powder

Serve immediately, or place truffles into air-tight container and store in fridge for up to 1 week.
