# Winnie’s Chocolate Chip Cookies

> source: master.docx · section: BAKING + TREATS

## Ingredients

- 250g butter
- 2 eggs
- 2 cups flour
- 1 cup chocolate chips
- 2 cups sugar (1white, 1 brown)
- 1 tsp vanilla essence
- 2 tsp baking powder

## Method

Cream butter and sugar.

Add eggs and vanilla and mix.

Add flour and baking powder and chocolate chips (add more choc chips if you like).

Put spoonfuls onto a greased baking tray and cook for 15 minutes or until golden at 180 degree C / gas 4.
