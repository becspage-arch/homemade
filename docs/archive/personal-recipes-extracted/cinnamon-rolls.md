# Cinnamon Rolls

> source: master.docx · section: BAKING + TREATS

## Ingredients

### Filling

- 1/6 cup butter, softened
- 1/6 cup cream cheese
- 1 cup soft brown sugar
- 3 teaspoons cinnamon
- Rolls
- 2 cups flour
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ½ cup butter
- 1 egg
- 2/3 cup milk
- Icing (optional)
- 1/2 cup icing (confectioners) sugar
- Milk or cream

## Method

Cream all ingredients for filling.  Set aside.

Combine flour, sugar, baking powder and salt in food processor bowl.  Cut in butter until crumbly (or cut in by hand or prepare with a pastry blender).

Whisk egg and milk together.  Add all at once to the dry ingredients and whirl briefly to combine.

Turn dough out onto a floured surface and roll or pat into a 9 or 10 inch square.  Spread filling over, and roll up like a jelly roll.  Cut into 12 slices.

Arrange rolls in a greased 9 inch square baking pan.  Bake at 400 degrees F for 20 to 25 minutes.  Remove from pan.

Icing – Combine icing sugar with enough milk or cream to make a thin glaze.  Drizzle over the rolls.
