# Lentil & Feta Salad

> source: master.docx · section: LUNCHES · (serves 2)

## Ingredients

- ½ a 400g tin of black or green lentils, drained
- 1 spring onion, finely sliced
- 60g feta or cheddar, crumbled
- Handful of salad leaves, such as rocket or cos
- ½ tsp dried thyme
- Optional - Pinch of chilli flakes
- Dressing - 1 tbsp Balsamic vinegar & 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil

## Method

Mix all ingredients together in a bow and season to taste

Whisk together the oil & vinegar to make the dressing and drizzle over the salad.
