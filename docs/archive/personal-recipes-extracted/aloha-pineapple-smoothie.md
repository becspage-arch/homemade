# Aloha Pineapple Smoothie

> source: master.docx · section: SMOOTHIES

## Ingredients

- 1 cup pineapple cubed
- 1 cup strawberries quartered
- 1 banana sliced
- 1 cup low-fat vanilla greek yoghurt
- 1 1/2 cups ice
- 1/3 cup pineapple or apple juice

## Method

Place all ingredients in the blender and puree until smooth.
