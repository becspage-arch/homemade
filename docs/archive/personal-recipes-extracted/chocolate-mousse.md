# Chocolate Mousse

> source: master.docx · section: BAKING + TREATS

## Ingredients

- 1 frozen ripe banana (peeled)
- 3 tbsp cacao powder
- 1 avocado
- 2 tbsp raw honey
- 1 tsp lemon juice
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 2 tbsp water
- A tiny pinch of salt

## Method

Blitz all together, serve straight away or pop in fridge for later.
