## Method

Preheat the oven to  425 F (220 C / 200 Fan / Gas 7).

Prepare the spice mix by mixing the following in a large bowl: garlic, ginger, ground almonds, tomato puree, ground cumin, garam masala, ground coriander, turmeric, paprika, ground cardamom, ground cinnamon, white sugar and salt. Put aside.

Put chicken breast on an oven tray and bake in the oven for 10-12 minutes. It should be nearly cooked through but not quite.

Meanwhile, fry the onion in a pan for 5 minutes with the oil, stirring often until softened.

Remove chicken from the oven and put onto a clean plate. Use two forks to shred.

Add shredded chicken to the frying pan with the onion. Fry for approx 5 minutes until the chicken is cooked through and slightly browning.

Add the spice mix to the frying pan and stir to coat.

Add the yoghurt, ground almonds, coconut cream and sugar.

Stir everything together and bring to a gentle oil.

Simmer gently for 5-10 minutes to deepen the flavors. Add a little water to thin if it gets too thick. Taste to check seasoning and add salt & pepper as needed.

Serve with rice as desired.
