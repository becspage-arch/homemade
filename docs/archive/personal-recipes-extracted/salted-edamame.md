# Salted Edamame

> source: master.docx · section: SIDES

## Ingredients

- Edamame/Soybeans fresh or frozen
- Sea Salt to Taste

## Method

Bring a pot of water to a rapid boil.

Add soybeans and boil for 5 minutes.

Drain soybeans and put soybeans in a bowl.

Sprinkle salt on top and enjoy your edamame!
