# Celeriac and Apple Soup

> source: master.docx · section: LUNCHES · (Serves 4)

## Ingredients

- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 onion, chopped
- 1 leek, sliced
- 2cm root ginger, chopped
- 1 celeriac, peeled & chopped
- 4 apples, cored and quartered
- Large pinch of thyme leaves
- 1L vegetable stock
- 1L chicken stock (or substitute vegetable stock so you’re using 2L stock in total)
- Salt & pepper to taste
- 200ml creme fraiche
- Optional - 4 slices of bacon

## Method

Heat the oil in a large saucepan

Add onion, leek and ginger and chook over a medium heat for 10 mins until they are soft.

Stir in the celeria, apples & thyme and cook for a few more minutes.

Pour in the stock and some seasoning and simmer for 30 more minutes or until celeriac is tender.

When the celeriac is tender, remove the soup from the heat and use a hand blender or food processor to blitz it until it is smooth.

Stir in ½ the creme fraiche.

Optional - Cut the bacon into roughly 1cm pieces and fry until crispy.

Serve, dolloping the remainder of the creme fraiche into each bowl. Scatter bacon on top.
