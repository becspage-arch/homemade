# Ambrosia Salad (Not Dairy-Free)

> source: master.docx · section: DESSERTS

## Ingredients

- 1 punnet chopped strawberries
- 1 punnet raspberries
- 1 punnet blueberries
- 1 punnet boysenberries
- 2 chopped bananas
- 1 pot double cream
- 1 pot natural yoghurt
- 4 crumbled flake bars

## Method

Whip cream.

Mix in yoghurt.

Mix in all fruits.

Mix in most of the crumbled flake bars.

Put in a serving bowl.

Sprinkle the remainder of the flake bar over the top.

Serves 8-10.
