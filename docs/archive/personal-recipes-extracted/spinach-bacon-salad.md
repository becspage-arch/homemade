# Spinach & Bacon Salad

> source: master.docx · section: SIDES

## Ingredients

- Bacon
- Olive oil
- Baby spinach, washed
- Mayonnaise (or milk-free mayo)
- Parmesan

## Method

Cook bacon until partially cooked

Slice bacon into small chunks

Finish frying until crisp

Put mayonnaise in an bowl, pour on some mayo, mix until combined

Optionally stir large pinch of grated parmesan cheese through spinach

Serve with bacon sprinkled on top
