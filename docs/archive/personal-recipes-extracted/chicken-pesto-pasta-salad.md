# Chicken Pesto Pasta Salad

> source: master.docx · section: DINNERS

## Ingredients

- 1 pound penne pasta
- 1 (12-ounce) jar roasted red peppers, drained and roughly chopped
- 1 (6-ounce) jar basil pesto
- 1 cup shredded parmesan cheese
- 3 cups fresh arugula
- 2-3 chicken breasts, cooked and chopped (grilled)

## Method

Cook pasta according to package Method. Drain and drizzle with a little olive oil so pasta doesn’t stick. Let cool slightly (so it doesn’t melt the parmesan and make it all clumpy).

In a large serving bowl, toss pasta with the remaining ingredients until combined. Refrigerate for at least an hour, then serve.

Serves 8.
