# Cinder Toffee Ice Cream (Not Dairy-Free)

> source: master.docx · section: DESSERTS

## Ingredients

- Groundnut or sunflower oil for the tin
- 200g caster sugar
- 4 tbsp golden syrup
- 2 tsp bicarbonate of soda
- 397g tin sweetened condensed milk
- 2x 600ml pots double cream
- 2 tsp vanilla extract

## Method

lightly oil a non-stick baking sheet

To make the cinder toffee, heat the sugar and syrup gently in a large deep saucepan until the sugar melts and turns into a syrup, and then boil the mixture until it turns a deep, golden caramel

Whisk in the bicarbonate of soda (this will make it foam up) then quickly pour it onto the oiled baking sheet and cool. Break into bite-sized pieces.

Put the condensed milk, cream and vanilla into a large bowl. Beat with an electric whisk until softly whipped.

Fold in the pieces of cinder toffee.

Tip into a freezer container, cover and freeze.
