# Chicken Caesar Salad

> source: master.docx · section: LUNCHES · (serves 2)

## Ingredients

- 2 large heads of romaine lettuce
- 4 slices of streaky bacon
- 1 avocado
- 2 chicken breast cutlets

### Caesar dressing

- Optional croutons

## Method

Bake the chicken for 10-15 min until mostly cooked.

While this is baking, fry the bacon until crispy.

Shred the chicken with forks then fry until crispy.

Cut avocado into pieces.

Toss salad in dressing.

Serve with salad, chicken, parmesan, avocado, bacon tossed together. Add optional croutons.
