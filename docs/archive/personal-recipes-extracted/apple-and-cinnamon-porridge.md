# Apple and Cinnamon Porridge

> source: master.docx · section: BREAKFASTS

## Ingredients

- 25g rolled oats
- 175ml semi-skimmed milk
- 1 apple, grate
- ½ tsp ground cinnamon
- Pinch of salt
- Optional toppings - maple syrup, walnut pieces, yoghurt, caramelised apple pieces

## Method

Combine all ingredients in a saucepan.

Heat gently while stirring frequently.

Bring to the boil then simmer gently for 3-4 minutes, stirring frequently so it doesn’t stick.

Serve with any toppings
