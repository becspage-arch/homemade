# Cheddar Broccoli Rice Cups

> source: master.docx · section: SIDES

## Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon of salt
- ½ grounded garlic
- 1 cup of milk
- 2 eggs fully stirred
- ½ of flour
- 2 ½ tablespoons of cooking oil
- 1 ibis of butter (or vegan butter)
- 1 cheese without bones (or vegan cheese)

## Method

Make your ingredients ready by ensuring everything you need to use is available.

Heat the oven to 330 degrees. This helps to cook your meal to the required temperature and evenly distributes heat.

Softly oil the whole part of the cooking bowl. Ensure that the pane it’s approximately 8x 5 inches.

Place the broccoli in hot water and let it bake for about 7 to 9 minutes until it cools down to its best level. You will need to dry it with a towel if required.

Liquefy butter to low warmth and place it inside a large pot. Mix this spice with flour and salt and continue mixing them until they get soft. Gently top up the milk and stir them till they turn thick then start boiling for 2 minutes and detach them from the heat.

Crease the cheese and broccoli then mix them softly until the cheese gets warm. Top the eggs then stir them smoothly in order to combine.

Place the fully cooked dish and cook them for about 30- 40 minutes. Let them out from the oven, settle them for 4 minutes then serve and enjoy.

You can prefer using Broccoli and cauliflower.
