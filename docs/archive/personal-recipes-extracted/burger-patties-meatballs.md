# Burger Patties & Meatballs

> source: master.docx · section: DINNERS

## Ingredients

- 1 lb 450 grams mince beef
- ½ cup mayonnaise
- ¼ cup grated parmesan
- ¼ cup chopped spring onions
- 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- Salt & pepper to taste

## Method

Mix all ingredients in a blow.

Either form mixture into 4 patties,  or roll into meatballs

Cook on a hot frying pan for 4-5 minutes per side (or 10 mins for meatballs) until nicely caramelised on the outside.
