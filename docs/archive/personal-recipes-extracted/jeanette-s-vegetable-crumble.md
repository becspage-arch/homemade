# Jeanette’s Vegetable Crumble

> source: master.docx · section: DINNERS

## Ingredients

- 1 med. leek
- 2or 3 sticks of celery
- As much or as little cabbage as you want
- Onion
- Salt & pepper

## Method

Cook all the veg together until tender.

Make a white sauce, add to veg.

Transfer to oven proof dish.

Cover with breadcrumbs, bake for about 30 minutes.
