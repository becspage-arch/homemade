# Vanessa’s Quiche

> source: master.docx · section: LUNCHES

## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 4 tablespoons milk (try with no milk or oat milk)

### Filling – eg spinach, ham, spring onion

- Grated cheese or vegan cheese
- Puff pastry

## Method

Assemble

Bake at 180 (gas 5) for 15 mins.

Lower temperature to 150 (gas 4) for a further 15 mins
