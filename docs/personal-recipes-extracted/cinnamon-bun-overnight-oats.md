# Cinnamon Bun Overnight Oats

> source: master.docx · section: BREAKFASTS

## Ingredients

- ⅔ cup plain Greek yoghurt
- 1 ⅓ cup cashew milk
- ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
- ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
- a pinch of salt
- 2 tablespoons chia seeds
- 2 tablespoons brown sugar ((or other sweetener, to taste))
- 1 cup rolled oats
- ¼ cup sultana raisins

## Method

In a large bowl, mix together the liquids.

Add the remaining ingredients and mix together.

Either portion the oats into jars and add lids, or place the entire portion in a large container and add a lid.  Refrigerate overnight.

Top with whipped heavy cream, if preferred.  You can eat these oats hot or cold.
