# Vegan Peanut Butter Cookies

> source: master.docx · section: BAKING + TREATS

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup peanut butter, or allergy friendly sub
- 2 tbsp applesauce (or make these Keto Peanut Butter Cookies)
- 3/4 tsp baking soda
- 3 tbsp flour (spelt, white, oat, sorghum, rice all work)
- 1/2 tsp pure vanilla extract
- 1/3 cup sugar, unrefined if desired
- 1/8 tsp salt

## Method

Combine dry ingredients in a mixing bowl.

If your peanut butter is not stir-able, gently warm to soften.

Add all remaining ingredients to the mixing bowl and stir to form a dough.

Roll into cookie balls.

If you want soft cookies, refrigerate the dough for at least an hour. (You can also freeze cookie dough balls to bake at another time if you wish.)

Bake in a preheated oven (350F) for 8 minutes.

The cookies will look quite underdone when you take them out.

Let cool for at least 10 minutes before removing from the tray, as they firm up while cooling.
