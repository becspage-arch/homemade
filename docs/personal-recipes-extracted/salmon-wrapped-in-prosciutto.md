# Salmon Wrapped In Prosciutto

> source: master.docx · section: DINNERS

## Ingredients

- 200g small new potato, sliced
- 3 sprigs tarragon, leaves chopped
- zest 1 lemon
- knob of butter
- 2 x skinless salmon fillet
- 4 slices pancetta or prosciutto
- Green beans

## Method

Heat oven to 200C/fan 180C/gas 6.

Boil the potatoes for 5 mins.

Drain and tip into a bowl - they will be slightly underdone.

Toss with the tarragon, lemon zest, butter and seasoning to taste. Pile in the centre of a foil-lined baking tray.

Season salmon with black pepper and wrap the pancetta or prosciutto around the fillets.

Place on top of the potatoes and roast for 15-20 mins, or until the fish flakes easily and the pancetta or prosciutto is golden.

Serve with steamed green beans.
