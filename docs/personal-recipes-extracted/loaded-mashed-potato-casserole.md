# Loaded Mashed Potato Casserole

> source: master.docx · section: SIDES

## Ingredients

- 5 lb organic potatoes peeled and sliced
- 3/4 cup sour cream (or dairy-free sour cream)
- 1 cup milk (or almond or oak milk)
- 5 tablespoons butter (or vegan butter)
- salt & pepper to taste
- 3 cups medium Cheddar cheese shredded (or vegan cheese)
- 8 slices bacon baked in the oven 400F for 10 min,

## Method

First, place bacon on a baking sheet lined with foil paper and bake at 400F for 10 min or until crispy texture, after finely chop bacon into tiny pieces for garnish & set aside.

While bacon is baking, peel and slice 5 lb potatoes and place them into a big pot. Cover potatoes with water and bring them to a boil. After the potatoes start boiling, reduce the heat to medium/high and cook for about 10-15 minutes or until potatoes turn soft when inserted with a knife.

Preheat the oven to 350 F.

Drain water and place potato in a big mixing bowl. Now add 3/4 cup sour cream, 1 cup milk, 5 tablespoons of butter, salt and pepper to taste. Blend with a blender until fluffy texture.

Using a spatula, fold in 2 cups of cheese, then transfer potatoes to a casserole dish, top with 1 cup of shredded cheese and bake uncovered at 350 F for 30 minutes or until heated thru.

Garnish potato casserole with crumbled bacon pieces and finely chopped fresh chives. Enjoy!

Making ahead - Make this potato casserole ahead by following step 1-3, then cover the casserole and place it in the fridge until you are ready to bake it.  About 30 minutes before serving follow steps 4-5.
