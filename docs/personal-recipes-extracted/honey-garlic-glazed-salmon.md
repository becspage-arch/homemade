# Honey Garlic Glazed Salmon

> source: master.docx · section: DINNERS

## Ingredients

- Salmon
- 4 (6 oz each) salmon fillets
- ½ tsp kosher salt
- ½ tsp black pepper
- ½ tsp smoked paprika (or regular paprika)
- ¼ tsp blackening seasoning (optional)

### Sauce

- 3 Tbsp butter
- 2 tsp olive oil
- 6 cloves garlic minced
- ½ cup honey
- 3 Tbsp water
- 3 Tbsp soy sauce
- 1 Tbsp sriracha sauce
- 2 Tbsp lemon juice

## Method

Pat salmon dry, then season with salt, pepper, paprika and blackening seasoning (if using). Set aside.

Adjust oven rack to middle position, then preheat broiler/grill.

Add butter and oil to a large, oven-safe skillet over MED-HIGH heat.

Once butter is melted, add garlic, water, soy sauce, sriracha, honey and lemon juice and cook for 30 seconds or so, until the sauce is heated through.

Add salmon, skin side down (if using salmon with skin), and cook for 3 minutes. While salmon cooks, baste frequently with sauce from the pan by spooning it over the top of the salmon.

Broil/grill salmon for 5-6 minutes, basting with sauce once during the broil, until salmon is caramelised and cooked to desired doneness.

Garnish with minced parsley if desired.
