# Seed Bar

> source: master.docx · section: BAKING + TREATS

## Ingredients

- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 tsp mixed spice
- 100g butter
- 1/2 cup sesame seeds
- 1/2 cup apricot jam
- 1/2 cup coconut
- 1/4 cup golden syrup
- 1 tsp vanilla essence
- 3 cups rolled oats
- 1 cup sultanas (optional)
- 1 cup pumpkin kernels

## Method

In a saucepan, melt together the sugar, butter, apricot jam and golden syrup.

Combine the rest of the ingredients in a large bowl. Mix well. Pour in melted mixture. Mix together. Press firmly into a greased 20x30cm baking tray.

Bake at 180c for 25 minutes or until golden.

Cool slightly and cut into bars. Makes 18.
