# Hot Cross Buns

> source: master.docx · section: BREAD

## Ingredients

- 300ml milk
- 500g strong white flour
- 75g caster sugar
- 1 tsp salt
- 7g sachet fast yeast
- 50g butter
- 1 egg, beaten
- 150g sultanas
- 80g mixed peel
- 1 apple, chopped
- 2 oranges zest
- 2 tsp cinnamon
- Crosses
- ½ cup (75g) flour (any white flour)
- 5 tbsp water

## Method

Bring milk to boil.

Let it cool to ‘hand’ temperature…. do this first. It takes AGES to cool!!

Mix flour, sugar, salt, butter, yeast and egg together whilst slowly adding milk until soft, sticky dough forms.

Add sultanas, peel, apple, zest & cinnamon.

Knead for 10 mins.

Put in a greased bowl & cover.

Prove for 1 hour.

Divide into round balls (75-80g each) on a paper-lined baking tray.

Cover and allow to prove for another hour.

Mix flour and water for crosses until a thick runny paste forms.

Spoon into a round 3 mm piping bag or small ziplock bag then snip the corner.

Remove the cling wrap from the buns and pipe crosses onto the buns. Go slow so it hugs the curves.

Bake at 200 C for 15-20 minutes

Glaze with apricot jam.
