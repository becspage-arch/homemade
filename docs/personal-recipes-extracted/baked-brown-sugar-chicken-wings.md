# Baked Brown Sugar Chicken Wings

> source: master.docx · section: DINNERS

## Ingredients

- Chicken
- 2 pounds chicken wings
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 2 teaspoons brown sugar
- 2 teaspoon garlic powder
- 2 teaspoon chilli powder
- 2 teaspoon smoked paprika
- 1 teaspoon onion powder
- Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste

### Sauce

- ⅓ cup roasted red pepper, chopped
- 2 ounces goat cheese (or vegan alternative)
- 2 tablespoons sour cream (or vegan alternative)
- ¼ teaspoon salt

## Method

Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F. Line a baking sheet with foil.

To make the dipping sauce, combine roasted red pepper, goat cheese, sour cream and salt in the bowl of a food processor until emulsified; set aside.

In a large bowl, combine wings, olive oil, brown sugar, garlic powder, chilli powder, paprika, onion powder, salt and pepper, to taste.

Place wings onto the prepared baking sheet and bake for 25 minutes, using metal tongs to turn at halftime.

Serve wings immediately with dipping sauce.
