# Stewed Fruit Compote

> source: master.docx · section: BREAKFASTS

## Ingredients

- 150ml water
- 150ml caster sugar
- 2x cloves
- Star anise
- ¼ stick cinnamon
- Vanilla
- Pears
- Apples
- Raspberries

## Method

Put water, sugar & spices in a pot.

Bring to the boil.

Boil until clear then take the spices out.

Put pears in and simmer for approximately 5 minutes until slightly softer than they were when they went in.

Put apples in and simmer until softer, approximately 3-5 minutes.

Put a handful of raspberries in and simmer 1-2 minutes.

Take off the heat and cool.

Store in the fridge. Serve with yoghurt, porridge or ice cream.
