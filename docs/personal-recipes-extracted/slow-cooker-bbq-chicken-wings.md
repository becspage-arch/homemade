# Slow Cooker BBQ Chicken Wings

> source: master.docx · section: DINNERS

## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon brown sugar
- 1 tablespoon sweet paprika
- 1 teaspoon dry mustard
- 1 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 teaspoon onion powder
- ¼ teaspoon cayenne pepper
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon pepper
- 4 pounds chicken wings and drummettes, or chicken drumsticks, trimmed. See Note
- 1 ½ cups barbecue sauce, divided
- Vegetable oil spray

## Method

Mix sugar, paprika, mustard, cumin, garlic powder, onion powder, cayenne, salt, and pepper together. Place ingredients in a large plastic zip-lock bag. Dump chicken wings or drumsticks into a bag and shake until all chicken is coated; transfer to slow cooker. Pour ½ cup of the barbecue sauce over chicken and stir to coat. Cover and cook until chicken is tender, 3-4 hours on low for wings, 4-6 hours on low for drumsticks.

Position oven rack 10 inches from broiler element and heat on high. Place a wire cooling rack on an aluminium foil-lined rimmed baking sheet and coat the wire rack with vegetable oil spray. Transfer chicken to the prepared baking sheet; discard braising liquid that remains in the slow cooker.

Brush chicken with ½ cup more barbecue sauce and broil until lightly charred and crisp (to your liking), about 10 to 15 minutes. Flip chicken over, brush with remaining ½ cup barbecue sauce, and continue to broil until lightly charred and crisp on the second side, 5 to 10 minutes longer. Serve.

Note about Drumsticks - I've made these with wings and drumsticks and the wings do turn out much more juicy and have more flavour. Drumsticks are good, but we all prefer the wings.
