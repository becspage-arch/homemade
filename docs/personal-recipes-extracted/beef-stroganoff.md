# Beef Stroganoff

> source: master.docx · section: DINNERS · (serves 2)

## Ingredients

- 250g sirloin steak
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 25g butter
- 1 medium online, peeled and thinly sliced
- 150 chestnut mushrooms, sliced
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 175ml beef stock (made with 1x full beef stock cube)
- Approx 2 tbsp full-fat creme fraiche (approx 30g)

## Method

Trim away any fat from the steak, place into a plastic (food grade) bag and tenderise. I do this hitting the bag a number of times with a rolling pin.

Take the steak out of the bag and slice into thin strips, not more than 1cm wide (I usually do more like ½ cm wide - thinner means they get more covered in the sauce!)

Add the olive oil, butter, onion and mushrooms to a frying pan and fry until the onions are soft and mushrooms caramelising.

Add the steak and fry until the meat is browned.

Add the paprika and stir to combine (if needed, temporarily remove from the heat while you stir so it doesn’t catch. The paprika will burn relatively easily so you need to stir it in quickly).

Add the beef stock and simmer for a few minutes until about half of it has absorbed or evaporated.

Add the cream fraiche and mix in.

Simmer for another 3-4 mins, stirring frequently, for the creme fraiche to simmer down. Serve.
