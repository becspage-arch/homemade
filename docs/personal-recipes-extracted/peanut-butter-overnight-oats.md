# Peanut Butter Overnight Oats

> source: master.docx · section: BREAKFASTS

## Ingredients

- ½ cup almond milk
- ⅓ cups rolled oats (I used gluten-free)
- 1 teaspoon honey (or sweetener of choice)
- 1 Tablespoon creamy peanut butter

## Method

Mix all of the ingredients together in a small mason jar or bowl. Do not cook the oats, add them dry (they will soak up the liquid overnight).

Close the jar with a lid or cover bowl with plastic wrap.

Place in the fridge overnight.

The next morning, top with sliced bananas or berries, peanut butter, honey and chocolate chips if desired. Serve cold.

Notes

Overnight oats will keep in the fridge for up to 1 week.

Right before enjoying it, I like to add an extra splash of almond milk to keep oats creamy and moist.
