# Ham and Cream Cheese Wraps

> source: master.docx · section: SIDES

## Ingredients

- 200g cream cheese, at room temperature
- 100g sour cream
- 2 tablespoons chopped chives
- 2 cloves of garlic
- Cracked pepper to taste
- 12 slices deli sliced ham, sliced thin enough to easily roll into a tube

## Method

Mix cream cheese, sour cream, sweet onion, chives, cracked pepper and finely chopped garlic. Spread cheese mixture on ham slices. Roll ham slices into logs. Refrigerate at least 1 hour. Cut ham wraps into thirds for serving as an entree or into 1-inch thick spirals to serve as an appetizer.

Wrap each ham roll in a leaf of lettuce for an easy sandwich.

Yield: 6 servings as an entree, 12 servings as an appetizer.
