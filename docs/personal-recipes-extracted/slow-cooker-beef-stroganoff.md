# Slow Cooker Beef Stroganoff

> source: master.docx · section: DINNERS

## Ingredients

- 2 pounds beef stew meat
- 8 ounces mushrooms sliced (optional)
- 2 packets (1 ounce, each) Lipton onion soup mix
- 1 cup low sodium beef broth
- 1/4 cup Worcestershire sauce
- 1 container (16 ounces) sour cream (or vegan sour cream)
- 5 ounces chive and onion cream cheese (or vegan cream cheese)
- 2/3 cup heavy whipping cream (or full fat coconut milk from a can)
- black pepper to taste
- 12 ounces cooked egg noodles

## Method

Spray the inside of a large slow cooker with cooking spray (I use a 7 quart size slow cooker).

Place the stew meat, mushrooms (if using), onion soup mix, beef broth and Worcestershire sauce in a slow cooker. Cover with lid and cook on low heat for 5 to 6 hours.

In a medium bowl combine sour cream, cream cheese and heavy whipping cream. Whisk to combine then stir into meat mixture. Season with a little pepper.

Cover with lid and let it warm up for another 20 minutes.

Serve over cooked egg noodles and enjoy!
