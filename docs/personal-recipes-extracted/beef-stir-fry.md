# Beef Stir Fry

> source: master.docx · section: DINNERS · (serves 2)

## Ingredients

- 200g beef fillet, thinly sliced
- 3 tbsp soy sauce
- 2cm root ginger, grated
- 1 garlic clove, finely chopped
- ½ tbs mild chilli powder
- 1 tbsp olive or coconut oil
- 150g green beans
- 150g mange tout or sugar snap peas
- ½ tsp sesame seeds

## Method

Mix together the soy sauce, ginger, garlic and chilli in a bowl

Add the beef, stir and leave to marinate for 30 minutes.

Mix in the beans & mange tout / sugar snap peas.

Stir fry over a high heat until the meat is browned, stirring frequently.

Keep frying for a few minutes more to caramelise it all, taking care to stir so it does not stick.

Add the sesame seeds, mix and serve.
