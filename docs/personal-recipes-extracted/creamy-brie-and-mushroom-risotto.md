# Creamy Brie and Mushroom Risotto

> source: master.docx · section: DINNERS

## Ingredients

- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 60g unsalted butter
- 2 leeks, finely chopped
- 2 garlic cloves, finely chopped
- 2 tbsp finely chopped rosemary leaves, plus extra leaves to serve
- 1 ⅓ cups (295g) arborio rice
- 100ml white wine
- 3 cups (750ml) Massel vegetable liquid stock, heated
- 100ml pure (thin) cream (or vegan alternative)
- 300g chestnut mushrooms
- 150g brie, torn (omit for vegan option)

## Method

Place oil and half the butter in a pan over medium-low heat.

Cook leek, garlic and half the rosemary, stirring, for 6-8 minutes until soft.

Add rice, stirring to coat grains, then add wine and simmer for 1-2 minutes or until almost evaporated.

Stir in stock, a ladleful at a time, allowing each to be absorbed before adding the next.

Continue to cook, stirring constantly, for 20 minutes or until al dente (you may not need all the stock).

Stir in cream and cook for a further 2-3 minutes until warmed through.

Melt remaining 30g butter in a pan over high heat.

Cook mushrooms and remaining rosemary for 6-8 minutes or until mushrooms are golden. Season.

Serve risotto immediately, topped with mushrooms, brie and rosemary leaves.
