# Chicken Wrapped in Parma Ham

> source: master.docx · section: DINNERS · (serves 2)

## Ingredients

- 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
- 2 tbsps full fat cream cheese
- 1 garlic clove crushed & finely chopped
- 6 slices of parma ham
- Olive oil

## Method

Preheat the oven to 180C.

Mix the cream cheese and garlic in a bowl. Season with salt & pepper.

Spread the mixture over the top of the chicken breasts

Wrap the chicken in parma ham. The ends of each breast will be sticking out, but the cheese mixture should be well covered.

Arrange on a tray or oven dish (this can end up messy if any mixture gets out so I use a higher sided tray, with baking paper under the chicken), drizzle with the olive oil, and bake for 30-35 mins.

Check the chicken is cooked inside (juices run clear), and parma ham nicely browned & crisp on the outside.

Serve with a salad or veges.
