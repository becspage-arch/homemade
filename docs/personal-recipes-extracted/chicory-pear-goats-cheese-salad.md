# Chicory, Pear & Goats Cheese Salad

> source: master.docx · section: LUNCHES · (serves 2)

## Ingredients

- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
- 2 heads of chicory
- 2 pears, cored and sliced
- 60g goats cheese, crumbled
- Handful of rocket leaves
- Small handful of pine nuts or toasted hazelnuts

## Method

In a bowl, make the dressing by whisking together the oil & vinegar with a small pinch of salt

Discard the outer leaves of the chicory then separate the rest from the stalk.

Arrange the leaves on the plates.

Sprinkle the rocket on top, then the pears, goats cheese and nuts on top.

Drizzle with the dressing.
