  Title: What Does My Guilt About This Say About My Worthiness Story?
  Type:  JOURNAL_PROMPT
  Mood:  (empty)
  First paragraph: A journal set for the guilt that arrives with money that wasn't earned. Inheritance, a gift from someone who had more, a legal settlement, a win, the common thread is that the usual transaction (effort for payment) didn't happen.   Slug:  the-complicated-grief-of-inheritance
  Title: The Complicated Grief of Inheritance
  Type:  READING
  Mood:  (empty)
  First paragraph: A reading on the specific difficulty of money that comes from loss. Inherited money is different from other unearned windfalls because the person who gave it is gone. The money and the grief arrive together, and they don't separate cleanly.

  Slug:  tapping-for-the-unearned-money
  Title: Tapping for the Unearned Money
  Type:  TAPPING
  Mood:  (empty)
  First paragraph: An EFT tapping sequence for the guilt that shows up when money arrives without the usual work attached to it. Inheritances, gifts, legal settlements, windfalls, the money is real, but something underneath makes it feel like it doesn't belong to you yet.
